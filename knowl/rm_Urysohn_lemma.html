Remark 1.21.3. Separation in normal spaces.

If the closed sets \(A, B\) are separated by the continuous function \(f\colon X\rightarrow [0,1]\text{,}\) they are separated by the open sets \(U=f^{-1}[0,1/2)\) and \(V=f^{-1}(1/2, 1]\text{.}\) The Urysohn lemma tells us that in normal spaces the two notions are equivalent!
