\begin{equation*} X=\{0,1\}^{\omega}=\prod_{i=1}^\infty \{0,1\}=\{(a_i)_{i=1}^n\colon a_i\in \{0,1\}\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  2. Compare the box and product topologies on \(X\text{,}\) where \(\{0,1\}\) is given the trivial topology.
