Given a separation \(X=U_1\cup U_2\text{,}\) we have \(Y=U_1'\cup U_2'\text{,}\) where \(U_i'=U_i\cap Y\text{.}\) Since \(U_1', U_2'\) are disjoint open subsets of the subspace \(Y\text{,}\) we have \(Y\subseteq U_1'\) or \(Y\subseteq U_2'\text{.}\) It follows that \(Y\subseteq U_1\) or \(Y\subseteq U_2\text{.}\)
