Remark 1.8.10. Nonequivalent norms.

Both the uniform metric \(\rho\) from Topological specimen 10 and the metrix \(D\) from Theorem 1.8.8 are induced by norms. Since they induce different topologies on \(\R^\omega\text{,}\) they furnish us with an example of two nonequivalent norms on an infinite-dimensional real vector space.
